

# The Acquisition Team — One-Page Advisory Website

## Overview
A professional, credible single-page website for a UK buy-side M&A advisory firm. The design will mirror the understated, corporate aesthetic of Business Exit Advisors — neutral tones, generous whitespace, clean typography, and zero startup energy. Mobile-first and trust-forward.

---

## Design System
- **Palette**: Deep navy/charcoal primary text, warm stone/off-white backgrounds, subtle gold or muted blue accents for CTAs — no bright colours
- **Typography**: Clean serif for headings (Georgia/similar), sans-serif for body text — institutional feel
- **Spacing**: Very generous padding between sections, lots of breathing room
- **Animations**: Minimal — subtle fade-ins only, nothing flashy

---

## Sections (in order)

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Logo text: "The Acquisition Team"
- Minimal nav links anchoring to page sections
- Sticky, clean, understated

### 2. Hero Section
- Large headline: "The Acquisition Team"
- Subheadline about fractional buy-side M&A execution
- Supporting description line
- Two buttons: "Book a Confidential Call" (primary) + "View Our Process" (text link)
- Subtle dark professional background with overlay

### 3. What We Do
- Section title with 7 concise bullet points covering origination through completion
- Factual, institutional tone — no marketing fluff
- Clean card or simple list layout

### 4. Our Deal Execution Process
- 5-step visual flow (horizontal on desktop, vertical on mobile)
- Steps: Origination → Screening → Modelling → Offer & Structure → Diligence & Close
- Each step has a short descriptor beneath
- Minimal design, like an advisory deck process graphic

### 5. Why Choose The Acquisition Team
- Opening paragraph about the value proposition
- 3 pillars displayed as clean cards or columns: Disciplined deal selection, Institutional execution, Buyer-aligned incentives
- No hype, credible tone

### 6. Engagement Options (Pricing)
- Two side-by-side cards: "Search Engine" (£1,500/mo) and "Execute One Deal" (£4,000 + 3%)
- Each with feature bullets and a CTA button
- Small footnote about partner/equity involvement
- Clean, professional card design — not SaaS-style pricing

### 7. Client Feedback (Testimonials)
- 3 understated testimonial cards with quotes and attribution
- No photos, no star ratings — just clean text
- Subtle background differentiation

### 8. Who We Are
- Brief credibility paragraph about the team's background
- Professional headshot placeholder image
- 3 optional credential bullets
- Trustworthy, understated presentation

### 9. Book a Confidential Strategy Call
- CTA section with headline, short description, and "Schedule a Call" button

### 10. Footer
